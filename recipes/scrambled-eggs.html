A Simple Egg Recipe

This is one of the easy ways to make eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 Tbsp milk
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Crack both eggs into a bowl
  2. Whisk eggs
  3. Whisk milk into the eggs
  4. Heat olive oil in small frying pan, on medium heat
  5. Add eggs to pan and scramble with spatula to desired texture
  6. Serve eggs with salt and pepper, to taste preference
