Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Photo by Griffin Wooldridge on Unsplash

Delicious Sandwich

A quick snack or meal

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of bread
  • 2 slices of choice of cheese
  • 1/2 Tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Melt butter in pan on medium-low heat
  2. Add 1 slice of bread to pan
  3. Put both slices of cheese on top of bread
  4. Add 2nd slice of bread on top of cheese
  5. Cook until cheese is melted, flipping sandwich once
